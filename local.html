Must Visit Landmarks

Jaipur Pink City Monument Guide

Amber Fort & Palace

Perched on the rugged Aravalli hills, this 16th-century red sandstone fortress features the mesmerizing Sheesh Mahal (Palace of Mirrors) and Maota Lake.

Hawa Mahal (Palace of Winds)

Built in 1799 by Maharaja Sawai Pratap Singh, this five-story pink sandstone beehive facade has 953 jharokha lattice windows designed for royal ladies.

City Palace & Jantar Mantar

The magnificent royal residence containing Mubarak Mahal, Chandra Mahal, and right beside it lies Jantar Mantar, housing the world's largest stone sundial.
