10 Sacred Shrines & Historic Hubs of Uttar Pradesh

Explore our directory of every spiritual and heritage destination serviced by our cabs. We provide door-to-door point transfers from Jaipur and Delhi NCR with experienced drivers.

01. Birthplace of Lord Ram

Ayodhya (Shri Ram Janmabhoomi)

The eternal sacred city along the banks of holy River Saryu. Home to the magnificent pink sandstone Ram Mandir, 10th-century Hanuman Garhi fortress, and golden-roofed Kanak Bhawan.

02. Eternal Spiritual Capital

Varanasi / Kashi (Vishwanath Corridor)

One of the oldest living cities on earth. Renowned for the golden-spired Kashi Vishwanath Jyotirlinga, grand evening Ganga Aarti at Dashashwamedh Ghat, and sunrise boat rides along 84 ghats.

03. Birthplace of Lord Krishna

Mathura (Shri Krishna Janmabhoomi)

Situated along the holy Yamuna River. Seek blessings inside the prison cell where Lord Krishna was born (Garbha Griha), and explore Dwarkadheesh Temple and Vishram Ghat Aarti.

04. Divine Krishna Playground

Vrindavan (Banke Bihari & Prem Mandir)

Where Lord Krishna spent his divine childhood. Famous for the lively darshan at Banke Bihari Mandir, illuminated white marble Prem Mandir, ISKCON temple, and mystical Nidhivan forest.

05. Braj Parikrama Hubs

Govardhan Hill & Barsana

Perform the sacred 21-km Govardhan Parikrama by cab or barefoot around Giriraj Parvat, and visit Shri Radha Rani Mandir perched atop Brahmagiri Hill in Barsana (birthplace of Radha).

06. Holy Confluence

Prayagraj (Triveni Sangam)

The most auspicious confluence of the three holy rivers: Ganga, Yamuna, and mythical underground Saraswati. Visit the reclining Bade Hanuman Ji temple and historic Anand Bhavan.

07. Buddhist Holy Sanctuary

Sarnath Stupa & Museum

Located just 10 km from Varanasi. Where Gautama Buddha delivered his first sermon after attaining enlightenment. Home to the towering Dhamek Stupa, Ashoka Pillar capital, and peaceful viharas.

08. UNESCO World Wonder

Agra (Taj Mahal & Agra Fort)

The crown jewel of Mughal architecture on the Yamuna riverbanks. Marvel at the pristine white marble Taj Mahal, red sandstone Agra Fort, Mehtab Bagh sunset views, and Akbar's tomb in Sikandra.

09. Abandoned Mughal Capital

Fatehpur Sikri

Constructed by Emperor Akbar as his capital in the 16th century (~40 km from Agra). Home to the colossal 54-meter Buland Darwaza, Salim Chishti's white marble dargah, and Panch Mahal.

10. City of Nawabs & Tehzeeb

Lucknow (Bara Imambara & Rumi Darwaza)

The historic capital of Uttar Pradesh known for courtly manners and culinary excellence. Explore the labyrinthine Bhool Bhulaiyaa at Bara Imambara, ornate Rumi Darwaza, and Hazratganj.
