Pickup by private AC vehicle from Jaipur or Jodhpur. Transfer to Bikaner (Camel Country). Visit the majestic red sandstone **Junagarh Fort**, Prachina Museum, and the famous world-unique **Karni Mata Rat Temple** in Deshnoke. Overnight stay in Bikaner.

Morning drive across the golden Thar Desert to Jaisalmer (330 km / 5.5 hrs). Check into your heritage hotel. Evening visit to **Gadisar Lake** and witness a mesmerizing desert sunset at **Vyas Chhatri** sandstone cenotaphs. Overnight in Jaisalmer.

Chauffeur welcomes you at Udaipur Airport or Railway Station. Transfer to lakeside hotel. Evening enjoy a romantic sunset boat cruise across **Lake Pichola**, visiting **Jag Mandir Island Palace** and viewing the majestic City Palace illuminated across the water.

Full day Udaipur sightseeing: visit the sprawling royal **City Palace Museum**, **Jagdish Temple**, and the royal fountain gardens of **Saheliyon Ki Bari**. Afternoon visit **Fateh Sagar Lake** and drive up to **Sajjangarh Monsoon Palace** for breathtaking sunset panoramic views over the Aravalli hills.

Morning drive to Mount Abu (165 km / 3.5 hrs). En route visit the world-famous **Ranakpur Jain Temple complex**, renowned for its 1,444 uniquely carved white marble pillars. Arrive in Mount Abu, check into hill station hotel, and relax at **Nakki Lake**.

Morning visit to the astonishing 11th-century **Dilwara Jain Temples**, featuring the world's finest marble craftsmanship. Drive up to **Guru Shikhar** (the highest peak in Rajasthan) and visit **Achalgarh Fort**. Evening stroll around **Toad Rock** and watch the dramatic sunset at **Sunset Point**.

Scenic drive down the Aravalli hills to **Kumbhalgarh Fortress**. Walk along its colossal 36-kilometer continuous wall (second longest wall in the world) and visit **Badal Mahal (Palace of Clouds)**. Enjoy the evening sound and light spectacle at the fort. Overnight stay at Kumbhalgarh resort.

Enjoy a relaxed breakfast amidst the green hills. Drive back to Udaipur (85 km / 2 hrs) where our driver drops you off at Udaipur Airport or Railway Station for your departure.

Drive across scenic Aravalli valleys to **Kumbhalgarh Fort** (birthplace of Maharana Pratap). Walk along the massive 36-km fortified ramparts and climb to **Badal Mahal** for panoramic views across the Mewar plains. Overnight stay at Kumbhalgarh.
