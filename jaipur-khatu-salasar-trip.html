Route Overview & Highlights

This sacred road trip takes you north-west of Jaipur through the scenic highways of Sikar and Churu districts. Designed specifically for families, senior citizens, and spiritual seekers, our experienced chauffeurs ensure smooth driving right up to the closest VIP parking gates at each shrine.

Step-by-Step Tour Itinerary

06:00 AM — Departure from Jaipur

Pickup from Your Home / Hotel / Airport

Our spotless, air-conditioned cab arrives right at your doorstep anywhere in Jaipur. Enjoy a serene early morning drive via the NH52 4-lane express highway toward Sikar with complimentary bottled water and soothing bhajans.

08:15 AM — 09:45 AM (Stop 1)

Darshan at Jeen Mata Temple (Sikar)

Situated around 115 km from Jaipur, Jeen Mata is our first sacred halt. Climb the gentle steps or opt for quick temple access to seek blessings of Goddess Jeen Bhavani. Enjoy fresh hot Kachoris and tea near the temple foothills.

10:30 AM — 01:30 PM (Stop 2)

Khatu Shyam Ji Temple & Shyam Kund

Drive 28 km further to the holy town of Khatu. Our driver guides you toward the nearest authorized parking lot. Perform sacred Parikrama, dip your hands at Shyam Kund, and experience the divine glory of Shyam Baba. Ample time allowed for Prasad shopping and lunch.

03:30 PM — 05:30 PM (Stop 3)

Salasar Balaji Hanuman Temple (Churu)

A smooth 105 km highway drive brings you to Salasar Balaji in Churu district. Tie the holy coconut (Nariyal Bandhan) at the sacred Dhuni tree for fulfillment of wishes, attend evening Aarti, and savor authentic Rajasthani Dal Baati Churma Prasad.

08:00 PM — 08:30 PM (Return)

Comfortable Drop-off back in Jaipur

Relax in the plush reclining seats of your cab as our skilled chauffeur navigates the return highway safely. Drop-off anywhere in Jaipur including Railway Station, Airport, or residential address.
