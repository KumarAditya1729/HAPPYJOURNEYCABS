11 Iconic Uttarakhand Shrines & Hill Hubs

Explore our detailed guide to every Himalayan destination serviced by Happy Journey Cabs. We provide round-trip and one-way drop services with experienced mountain drivers.

01. Gateway to God

Haridwar

Where the holy River Ganga descends from the mountains to the plains. Famous for the mesmerizing evening Ganga Aarti at Har Ki Pauri ghat, Mansa Devi ropeway, and Chandi Devi temple.

02. Yoga Capital of the World

Rishikesh

Nestled along the emerald waters of the Ganges. Celebrated for Ram Jhula, Laxman Jhula suspension bridges, Beatles Ashram, Triveni Ghat Aarti, and white-water river rafting.

03. Queen of the Hills

Mussoorie

Perched at 6,170 ft overlooking the Doon Valley. Famous for the bustling colonial Mall Road, Kempty Falls cascading pools, Gun Hill viewpoint, and misty pine tree slopes.

04. Lake District of India

Nainital

Built around the sacred eye-shaped Naini Lake surrounded by seven lush peaks. Enjoy paddle boating, Naina Devi Mandir darshan, Snow View cable car, and Tibetan market shopping.

05. Himalayan Ski Paradise

Auli Ski Resort

An alpine wonderland situated at 9,200 ft. Offers immaculate skiing slopes, panoramic vistas of Nanda Devi and Kamet peaks, and pristine artificial mountain reservoirs.

06. Premier Tiger Reserve

Jim Corbett National Park

India's oldest national park nestled along the Ramganga River. Famous for open jeep safaris across Dhikala and Bijrani zones to spot majestic Royal Bengal Tigers and wild elephants.

07. Highest Shiva Jyotirlinga

Kedarnath Dham

Dedicated to Lord Shiva, standing majestically at 11,755 ft near the Chorabari Glacier. Reached via 16-km trek from Gaurikund basecamp or helicopter from Guptkashi / Phata.

08. Sacred Vishnu Sanctuary

Badrinath Dham

Revered temple of Lord Vishnu situated along the Alaknanda River between Nar and Narayan mountain ranges. Pilgrims take holy dips in the thermal Tapt Kund before darshan.

09. Origin of Mother Ganga

Gangotri Dham

Located at 10,200 ft amidst deodar forests in Uttarkashi district. The white granite temple marks the spot where Goddess Ganga touched earth after King Bhagirath's penance.

10. Origin of River Yamuna

Yamunotri Dham

The westernmost shrine of the Char Dham circuit, located below Bandarpoonch peak. Famous for the boiling hot Surya Kund springs where rice is cooked as sacred temple prasad.

11. UNESCO Alpine Wonderland

Valley of Flowers & Hemkund Sahib

A breathtaking national park bursting with over 600 species of exotic alpine flowers every monsoon, situated right beside the high-altitude Sikh gurudwara of Sri Hemkund Sahib.
