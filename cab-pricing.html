Important Terms & Additional Charges

To maintain absolute clarity and trust with our guests, please note how outstation and local billing operates across our fleet:

  • Per Km Billing: Rates apply for round-trip outstation travel subject to a standard minimum billing limit (usually 250 km per day).
  • Toll Tax & Parking: Highway toll taxes, interstate permits, and monument/airport parking charges are billed separately as per actual receipts.
  • Driver Allowance: For overnight trips requiring stay outside Jaipur, a nominal chauffeur allowance (approx. ₹300/day) is applicable.
  • State Tax & Border Fees: When traveling across different state borders (e.g., from Rajasthan to UP or Delhi), actual border taxes apply.
  • Night Charges: Applicable only if the vehicle is driven between 10:00 PM and 6:00 AM on specific outstation routes.
  • No Hidden Surprises: We never charge fuel surcharges or unexpected booking fees. Your invoice matches our advance written estimate exactly.
