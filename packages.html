4.9 (3,200+ Trips) 15+ Years Experience

3-Day Jaipur Royal Heritage Tour

The perfect weekend introduction to the Pink City. Immerse yourself in magnificent Rajput forts, royal city palaces, astronomical marvels, and authentic village cultural dining.

Meet our royal chauffeur at Jaipur Airport or Railway Station. Transfer to your hotel. After lunch, visit the sprawling **City Palace**, royal **Jantar Mantar Observatory**, and take a photo stop outside the iconic **Hawa Mahal (Palace of Winds)**. Evening visit to **Birla Mandir** followed by dinner at traditional Rajasthani restaurant.

Morning excursion to the hilltop **Amber Fort** (optional elephant or jeep ascent). Visit **Panna Meena Ka Kund** stepwell and **Jal Mahal (Water Palace)** floating on Man Sagar Lake. In the evening, drive up to **Nahargarh Fort** for panoramic sunset city views, followed by cultural festivities at **Chokhi Dhani ethnic resort**.

After breakfast, visit **Albert Hall Museum** and **Patrika Gate**. Spend time shopping for authentic Jaipur blue pottery, bandhani textiles, and gemstone jewelry at Johari Bazaar and Bapu Bazaar. Chauffeur drops you at Airport/Station with sweet memories.

5-Day Golden Triangle & Royal Jaipur Tour

India's most famous travel circuit executed with supreme Rajasthani comfort. Explore the historical monuments of Delhi, the immortal Taj Mahal in Agra, and the regal palaces of Jaipur.

Pickup from New Delhi Airport/Hotel. Explore Old and New Delhi highlights: **Qutub Minar**, **Humayun's Tomb**, **India Gate**, **Lotus Temple**, and a drive past **Rashtrapati Bhavan**. Overnight stay in New Delhi.

Morning drive via Yamuna Expressway to Agra (210 km / 3.5 hrs). Check into hotel and visit the magnificent red sandstone **Agra Fort**. In the evening, witness the breathtaking sunset view of the **Taj Mahal** from Mehtab Bagh across the Yamuna. Overnight stay in Agra.

Early morning visit to inside the **Taj Mahal** during golden sunrise. Return for breakfast, check out, and drive towards Jaipur. En route explore **Fatehpur Sikri** (the abandoned Mughal capital) and the intricate **Chand Baori stepwell at Abhaneri**. Arrive in Jaipur by evening. Overnight stay in Jaipur.

Full day Jaipur tour covering **Amber Fort**, **Jal Mahal**, **Hawa Mahal**, **City Palace**, and **Jantar Mantar**. Evening dinner at Nahargarh Fort or local Rajasthani folk dance evening. Overnight in Jaipur.

Breakfast and souvenir shopping in Jaipur's royal bazaars. Chauffeur drops you off at Jaipur International Airport or drives you back to New Delhi Airport according to your departure flights.

7-Day Desert Jewel & Lakes Tour

Our best-selling classic Rajasthan itinerary covering the Pink City of Jaipur, the Blue City of Jodhpur, the Golden Sand Dunes of Jaisalmer, and the Romantic Lake City of Udaipur.

Complete 2-day exploration of Jaipur covering Amber Fort, Hawa Mahal, City Palace, Jantar Mantar, Nahargarh Fort, and Chokhi Dhani. Depart for Jodhpur on Day 2 morning via holy Pushkar Lake.

Arrive in Jodhpur. Explore the colossal **Mehrangarh Fort** towering over the indigo houses, visit **Jaswant Thada** marble cenotaph, and stroll through the bustling **Sardar Market Clock Tower**.

Drive to Jaisalmer (280 km / 4.5 hrs). Explore **Jaisalmer Golden Fort**, **Patwon Ki Haveli**, and **Gadisar Lake**. On Day 5 afternoon, transfer to **Sam Sand Dunes** for thrilling Jeep dune bashing, camel sunset safari, folk music night, and overnight stay in a Swiss Desert Camp under the stars.

Drive from Jaisalmer to Udaipur via the breathtaking 1,444 marble pillars of **Ranakpur Jain Temple**. In Udaipur, visit **City Palace**, **Saheliyon Ki Bari**, **Jagdish Temple**, and enjoy a sunset boat cruise on **Lake Pichola**. Day 7 evening drop at Udaipur Airport or Railway Station.

10-Day Complete Rajasthan Heritage & Hill Station Tour

A deep-dive expedition across Jaipur, the desert fortress of Bikaner, Jaisalmer dunes, Jodhpur blue city, the tranquil hill station of Mount Abu, and romantic Udaipur.

Explore Jaipur forts on Day 1. On Day 2, drive to **Bikaner**. Visit the unconquered red sandstone **Junagarh Fort** and the famous **Deshnoke Karni Mata Rat Temple**.

Drive to Jaisalmer across the Thar Desert. Explore Golden Fort, Havelis, and experience the complete 4x4 dune bashing and camel safari night at Sam Sand Dunes.

Drive to Jodhpur to explore Mehrangarh Fort and Umaid Bhawan Palace. On Day 7, proceed to Rajasthan's only hill station **Mount Abu**. Sunset boat ride on Nakki Lake and visit the world-famous marble carved **Dilwara Jain Temples**.

Drive to Udaipur via Ranakpur. Spend two full days exploring Lake Pichola, City Palace, and Sajjangarh Monsoon Palace. On Day 10, drive to Pushkar for Brahma Temple Darshan and final drop at Jaipur/Ajmer Station.

14-Day Grand Maharaja Odyssey (Delhi to Rajasthan & Agra)

The ultimate once-in-a-lifetime royal circuit of North India. Covers Delhi, painted havelis of Shekhawati (Mandawa), Bikaner forts, Jaisalmer dunes, Jodhpur blue city, Ranakpur, Udaipur lakes, Chittorgarh fort, Pushkar, Jaipur palaces, and Agra Taj Mahal.

Pickup in New Delhi. Drive to **Mandawa (Shekhawati)** on Day 2 to admire the world's largest open-air art gallery of fresco painted mansions. Day 3 drive to **Bikaner** for Junagarh Fort and Camel breeding farm.

Explore the living fortress of Jaisalmer, Jain temples, Patwon Ki Haveli, and spend two magical nights exploring both the city and the luxury Thar Desert camps at Sam Sand Dunes.

Explore Mehrangarh Fort and Jaswant Thada. En route to Udaipur, visit the world-renowned **Ranakpur Jain Temples** and Kumbhalgarh Fort (the second longest wall in the world after China). Spend 2 nights exploring Lake City Udaipur.

Visit the heroic **Chittorgarh Fort** (Vijay Stambha & Padmini Palace). Proceed to **Pushkar holy lake**. Spend Days 12 and 13 exploring the palaces and forts of **Jaipur**. On Day 14, visit **Agra Taj Mahal** before final drop at New Delhi International Airport.
