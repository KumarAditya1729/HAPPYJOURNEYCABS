How We Started

Founded over 15 years ago (Established 2011) in the heart of Jaipur, Happy Journey Cabs emerged from a simple yet powerful conviction: exploring the grand palaces, desert dunes, and sacred temples of Rajasthan should feel just as royal and effortless as the destinations themselves.

For over a decade and a half, travelers across India and abroad have trusted our transparent fares, verified royal chauffeurs, and spotless luxury fleet. Today, with 15+ years of unmatched hospitality, we stand proudly as Rajasthan's most veteran and trusted cab service.
