Frequently Asked Questions

How much advance booking is needed for Ranthambore Safari?

Ranthambore safari permits (especially 6-seater Gypsies for prime zones 1-5) get booked up 60 to 90 days in advance on the official Rajasthan forest portal (`fmdss.forest.rajasthan.gov.in`). Once your cab is confirmed with us, our 24×7 team will happily assist you in navigating online safari permit bookings or connecting with local authorized operators.

Can we book the cab for a 2-day / 3-day weekend overnight trip?

Yes, absolutely! The starting tariffs listed below are for a same-day roundtrip. If you wish to stay overnight at a resort in Sawai Madhopur, we offer transparent per-km / per-day weekend packages. Message us on WhatsApp for your custom overnight itinerary quote.

Can our private taxi drive up to Ranthambore Fort and Trinetra Ganesh?

Yes, private cars and taxis are permitted on the forest road up to the Ranthambore Fort parking area for visiting Trinetra Ganesh temple during daylight hours without needing a safari permit.
