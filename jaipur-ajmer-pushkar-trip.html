Route Overview & Highlights

Separated by just 14 km across the Nag Pahar (Snake Mountain) hills, Ajmer and Pushkar represent one of India's most unique interfaith pilgrimage circuits. Departing from Jaipur on the multi-lane Jaipur-Kishangarh-Ajmer expressway, our chauffeurs ensure a smooth 2.5-hour drive directly into the historic heart of Ajmer.

Ajmer Sharif Dargah

World-renowned Sufi shrine of Khwaja Moinuddin Chishti offering universal peace.

Pushkar Holy Lake & Ghats

Surrounded by 52 bathing ghats and hundreds of vibrant blue-and-white temples.

Lord Brahma Temple

The only prominent temple in the world dedicated to Jagatpita Lord Brahma.

Step-by-Step Tour Itinerary

07:30 AM — Departure from Jaipur

Doorstep Pickup from Hotel / Airport

Our chauffeur greets you with a clean AC car at your preferred time. Cruise along the NH8 Jaipur-Ajmer express highway, crossing Kishangarh (known for its marble industries and Kishangarh Dumping Yard photo spot upon request).

10:00 AM — 12:30 PM (Stop 1)

Ajmer Sharif Dargah & Akbari Fort

Arrive in Ajmer city. Our driver parks at the designated Dargah Bazaar parking and arranges reliable rickshaw/walking guidance to the main silver gates of Khwaja Garib Nawaz. Offer holy Chadar and experience the spiritual Qawwali atmosphere.

12:45 PM — 01:30 PM (Stop 2)

Ana Sagar Lake & Shah Jahan Pavilions

Stop by the picturesque marble pavilions (Baradari) overlooking the vast Ana Sagar Lake. Enjoy a refreshing walk along the promenade and capture scenic photos of the Aravalli hills reflecting on the water.

02:00 PM — 05:30 PM (Stop 3 & 4)

Pushkar Brahma Temple, Ghats & Bazaar Walk

Drive through the scenic mountain pass to holy Pushkar. Visit the sacred 14th-century Lord Brahma Temple with its red spire, perform Puja or water offering at Pushkar Lake Ghats, explore the colorful handicraft bazaar, and enjoy famous Pushkar Malpua and Gulkand Lassi.

06:30 PM — 08:30 PM (Return)

Comfortable Express Return to Jaipur

Depart Pushkar around sunset. Relax in air-conditioned comfort as we drive back on the illuminated NH8 highway, dropping you safely at your home, hotel, or Jaipur Airport.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the driving distance and time from Jaipur to Ajmer & Pushkar?

The driving distance from Jaipur to Ajmer is approximately 135 km (2.5 hours on the NH8 expressway). Pushkar is just 14 km further from Ajmer across a scenic hill pass. The entire same-day trip covers ~300 km roundtrip over 11 hours.

Can cars go right up to the Ajmer Sharif Dargah gate?

Due to narrow heritage bazaar lanes and crowd restrictions, cars are parked at the official Dargah Bazaar parking lot. From there, our chauffeur helps you take a convenient e-rickshaw or walk (~10 minutes) directly to the Dargah entrance.

Can we add Kishangarh Dumping Yard to this itinerary?

Yes, absolutely! Since Kishangarh is directly on the Jaipur-Ajmer expressway, we can easily include a 1-hour stop at the famous "Snowyard of Rajasthan" (Kishangarh Dumping Yard) with prior permission for a nominal customized charge.
